# AuditFlow

A corporate website scanner and compliance analyzer dashboard.

## Features

- **Website Audit Engine**: Scans domain metadata, legal pages, and tax details.
- **Compliance & Risk Rating**: Computes health scores and highlights potential risk factors.
- **Legal & Tax Verification**: Checks for Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, SSL, and Tax IDs (GSTIN/VAT/EIN).
- **Tech & Social Extraction**: Identifies CMS, analytics, payment rails, and social profiles.
- **Audit Reports**: Print and export audit summaries directly from the browser.
- **Dark / Light Theme**: Built-in toggle for comfortable viewing.

## Getting Started

Open `index.html` in any modern web browser to launch the app.
